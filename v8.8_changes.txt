Frostfall v8.8 — Palisade

- Walls now render as a connected palisade structure with neighbor-aware drawing.
- Single walls show a pointed wooden post with a snow cap.
- Adjacent walls (E/W) share a horizontal cross-beam and a continuous snow ridge.
- Adjacent walls (N/S) share a vertical connection beam so vertical runs read as one structure.
- Corners and T-junctions automatically combine horizontal and vertical connections.
- No new sprite assets — palisade is procedurally drawn on canvas.
- Friendlies (player, survivors, hunters) now walk through walls. Towers and ice towers still block.
- Enemies still respect walls and attack them as before.
- SAVE_KEY bumped to frostfall-save-v8-8-palisade with v8.7 added to legacy migration list.
- Service-worker cache bumped to frostfall-v8-8.
