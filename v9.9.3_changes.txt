v9.9.3 — Tower Caps Balance

- Added tower caps for regular towers and ice towers.
- Base game: max 25 regular towers and max 25 ice towers.
- New Game+: max 30 regular towers and max 30 ice towers.
- Palisades/walls remain unlimited.
- Existing above-cap towers are preserved, but additional builds are blocked.
- Build buttons show current count/cap.
- Build preview turns red when capped.
- Service-worker cache bumped to frostfall-v9-9-3.
