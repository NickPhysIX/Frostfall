# Frostfall v8.7 — Hunters & Compass

## New: Direction indicators

- Added subtle edge-of-screen directional arrows.
- White arrow points back toward camp/home when the camp is off-screen.
- Red arrows point toward off-screen enemies.
- Boss/alpha enemies get slightly stronger red/pink arrows and a small dot marker.
- Enemy arrows are capped at three at a time to avoid clutter.

## New: Hunters

- Added `hunter.png` as a new helper/NPC sprite.
- Added a new **Jagers** upgrade in Tactical Forge.
- Hunters share the same max cap as survivors: max 4.
- Hunters are permanent helpers and do not die.
- Hunters patrol near the camp when idle.
- Hunters automatically target enemies within a guard radius.
- At range, hunters fire a weak projectile attack.
- At close distance, hunters use a stronger melee/spinning-axe style attack.
- Hunter damage scales only from the player’s axe damage/attack power, not from a separate upgrade tree.
