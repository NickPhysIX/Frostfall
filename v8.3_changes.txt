Frostfall v8.3 — Audio Lifecycle Fix

- Added explicit audio lifecycle handling for Safari/iOS/PWA.
- Music and SFX now pause when the page/app is hidden, page-hidden, or frozen.
- Audio resumes only if it was actually playing before the app went to the background.
- Master mute remains authoritative: muted audio will not resume automatically.
- Active Web Audio one-shot SFX are stopped when backgrounding.
- Suspends Web Audio context while backgrounded to prevent lingering sound.
- Bumped SAVE_KEY to frostfall-save-v8-3-audio-lifecycle with v8.2 as legacy fallback.
- Bumped service-worker cache to frostfall-v8-3.
