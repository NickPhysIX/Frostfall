# Frostfall v8.4 — Brute Beasts

This release adds two new enemy variants and makes Yeti waves more interesting.

## New enemies

- **Brute Frost Wolf** — tougher and stronger than the normal Frost Wolf, but slower.
- **Brute Yeti** — a smaller, faster support version of the Yeti.

## Yeti wave change

Yeti waves now spawn one main Yeti plus a limited pack of 4–5 Brute Yetis. This should make Yeti waves feel more chaotic and tactical without flooding the screen endlessly.
