Frostfall v9.4 — Balanced Villager Repairs

- Limited automatic villager repairs to preserve resource gathering.
- Emergency repairs: structures <25% HP, max 2 villagers globally.
- Maintenance repairs: structures 25–50% HP, max 1 villager globally.
- Villagers protect a 25 wood reserve.
- Villagers repair in bursts: 4 ticks, 18 HP each, 1 wood per tick.
- Auto-repair stops around 70% HP; full repair remains player-controlled and costs money.
- SAVE_KEY and service worker cache bumped to v9.4.
