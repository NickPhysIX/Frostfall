# Frostfall v9.7.2 — Crow Heist Tuning

This update makes the Crow Thief mechanic more readable and fair. Previously crows could be killed during approach, which meant they often never reached the meat pile and the player might not notice the mechanic.

## Changes
- First crow check occurs after about 45 seconds once the player has 25+ meat.
- After a successful crow spawn, the next one is scheduled roughly 75–120 seconds later.
- If there is not enough meat, the game checks again after 10 seconds.
- Only one crow can be active at a time.
- Crows are invulnerable / ignored during approach.
- Crows become vulnerable only after stealing meat.
- Killing a meat-carrying crow returns the stolen meat as a drop.
- Bestiary text updated.
- Service-worker cache bumped to `frostfall-v9-7-2`.

No save reset required.
