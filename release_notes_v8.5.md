# Frostfall v8.5 — Exchange & Mobile QoL

This update focuses on late-game usability and mobile ergonomics.

## Added

- New **Exchange** tab in the Tactical Forge.
- Lossy resource conversions between wood, meat and money.

## Changed

- Upgrade/build buttons are slightly less close to the edge.
- Touch joystick is moved slightly left and upward.
- Pause and sound buttons are stacked under the wave/status panel.
- Service worker cache bumped to `frostfall-v8-5`.
- Save key bumped to `frostfall-save-v8-5-exchange-qol`.
