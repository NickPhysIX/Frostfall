# Frostfall v9.7.3 — Crow Escape Balance

## Summary

This update tunes the Crow Thief mechanic so the crow becomes a visible, readable little heist instead of disappearing immediately after reaching the meat pile.

## Included changes

- Crow no longer disappears immediately after stealing meat.
- After stealing, the crow visibly flies away with the stolen meat.
- Crow HP increased so the player has a fair chance to notice and shoot it down.
- Crow gets a short escape grace period after stealing before it becomes targetable/killable.
- Crow-caw SFX added as `crow_caw.mp3`.
- Crow caw plays 2–3 times when the crow steals meat.
- Existing mute control remains a master mute for music and SFX.
- Service-worker cache bumped to `frostfall-v9-7-3`.

## Save / compatibility

- No save reset required.
- Existing saves continue to work.
