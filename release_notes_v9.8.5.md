# Frostfall v9.8.5 — Sequential Mixed-Wave Audio Cues

## Summary

Mixed waves now communicate their composition better. Instead of only playing one animal sound for the first enemy in the wave, Frostfall now queues one cue for every unique enemy-cue result that appears during the wave.

## Changes

- Waves can now play multiple animal cues when multiple enemy families or differentiated brute variants appear.
- Cues are queued and played sequentially with at least 1.2 seconds of spacing, preventing overlapping howls/growls.
- The wave-start opener still uses the preselected first enemy kind, so the first cue remains immediate and accurate.
- Deduplication is based on `sfx + pitch`, allowing normal wolf and Brute Wolf cues to both play when they are meaningfully different.
- The old `waveAnimalCuePlayed` boolean has been replaced by `waveCuePlayedKeys`, `waveCueQueue`, and `waveCueQueueTimer`.
- The cue queue is flushed silently during mute/background state so queued cues do not burst after unmuting or returning.
- Yeti finale opening and Alpha Boss cues keep their dramatic direct/queued behavior with clean queue state per boss.
- Includes the v9.8.4 brute audio pitch differentiation.
- No save impact; service-worker cache bumped to `frostfall-v9-8-5`.
