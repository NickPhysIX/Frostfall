# Frostfall v9.9.2 — New Game+ Expanded Upgrades

## Summary
This update makes New Game+ more rewarding by adding a small post-victory progression layer.

After entering New Game+, capped upgrades can be pushed a little further, and the player can buy a few more permanent NPC helpers.

## Changes
- New Game+ unlocks **+2 extra levels** for capped upgrades.
- Survivors cap increases from 4 to 6 in New Game+.
- Hunters cap increases from 4 to 6 in New Game+.
- Upgrade cards now show `Lv.x/y` for capped upgrades.
- Uncapped upgrades such as axe damage and max HP remain uncapped.
- Service-worker cache bumped to `frostfall-v9-9-2`.

## Save / compatibility
- No save reset required.
- Existing saves remain compatible.
- Extra caps activate when `newGamePlusRuns > 0`.
